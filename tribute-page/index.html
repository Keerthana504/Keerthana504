A Tribute to Matthew Perry aka Chandler Bing

F.R.I.E.N.D.S is an American Sitcom that was aired in early 1990’s and was a successful running show for a decade and remains popular in this generation also.

Friends revolves around 6 group of 6 pals, that are neighbours in an apartment at NYC. They all gossip, chat and have coffees at a special place called Central Perk and this place is still known for the show.

"Friends" is known for its humor, sarcasm, catchphrases, relationships and adventures of the characters. It has has a dedicated fan base even after 2 decades of its last episode, making it one of the most iconic and beloved sitcoms in the television history. The show explores themes of friendship, love, and the ups and downs of young adulthood in a lighthearted and relatable manner.

In a personal note, I binge-watched Friends during the pandemic due to its innocence, unique concept, and extremely relatable journey to the present times of my life. I've seen the show several times over and yet never get tired of it.  I also bought a miniature of FRIENDS to cherish their memories at home. 

Friends will never feel the same now that Mathew Perry has passed away suddenly. Everybody feels an irreplaceable gap in their hearts. It is similar to losing a family member or close friend.

Chandler Bing - We continue to use the sarcastic jokes you left behind in our everyday conversations. We shall always carry you in our hearts.

"We will always cherish the joy, the light, the blinding intelligence he brought to every moment – not just to his work, but in life as well" --- Kauffman and Crane , Directors
