Hello, I'm Keerthana.

A Frontend Developer

Firstly, I began my career as an Analyst at Deloitte Consulting in India, where I initially worked on the Application Management for an insurance project that helped me gain knowledge on how a software company functions and its work culture. After working there for one and half years, I’ve got a good understanding of the software development life cycle and I developed an interest in frontend technologies and started taking on tasks such as debugging, solving website-related issues, and bug fixing.

I worked on various websites that use different tech stacks for the frontend. I’m thankful for this project because I have had opportunities to collaborate with different team leads and accomplish the tasks by utilizing my skills which helped in the significant growth of the team’s performance. I gained a lot of knowledge from my teammates and learned many new things about front-end development. I was a senior developer in the team, I was mentoring and onboarding the new teammates, I also provided KT on technologies and languages that aligned them with the team’s pace within a few weeks. I helped my juniors by providing them with right assistance.

I have received several awards and client appreciations. I was promoted twice in 6 years and was a consistent performer throughout my career at Deloitte by receiving Above and Exceptional performance reviews for most of the years.
